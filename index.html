Kaspa: The Next Generation Protocol

This comprehensive guide delves into Kaspa, a groundbreaking cryptocurrency that fundamentally redefines the principles of distributed ledger technology. Unlike traditional blockchains, Kaspa leverages a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) structure and the GHOSTDAG protocol to achieve unparalleled scalability, speed, and decentralization without compromise. Explore how Kaspa solves the "blockchain trilemma," its innovative pruning system, and its vision for future Layer 2 solutions. This book aims to demystify complex concepts, offering insights for developers, investors, and crypto enthusiasts alike.
